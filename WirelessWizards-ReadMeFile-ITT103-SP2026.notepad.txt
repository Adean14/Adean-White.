*****Program Purpose*****
Creating a user-friendly Point of Sale (POS) system that enables cashiers to handle customer purchases at Best Buy Retail Store efficiently.

*****Key Features*****
Product Management
stock
price 
display all products with their details

Add to Cart
select a product
entry quantity
checks if product exits
ensures quantity is valid
prevents exceeding stock 
automatically reduces stock when added to cart

Remove From Cart
let users remove items from their cart 
automatically restocks inventory when items are removed

Cart summary View
Displays - 
items in cart
quantity of each item
total cost per item
subtotal

Checkout System
calculates
subtotal
discount (5% if over 5,000)
tax (10%)
final total
calculates change
accept payment and check if sufficient

Receipt Generation
Prints a detail receipt including:
items purchased
payment
costs
change

Low Stock Alert
alert when stock is below a threshold (less than 5 items)

Continuous Operation (Main Menu Loop)
runs continuously until user exits
provides a menu-driven interface for ease of use

Bonus (Implicit Feature)
error handling and input validation
prevents crashes from invalid inputs
ensure smooth user experience

*****System*****
PyCharm 2025.3.1.1
